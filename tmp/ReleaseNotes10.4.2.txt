coDiagnostiX

            Release Notes 10.4.2
We’re constantly working to improve your experience with coDiagnostiX. Below you will find a
summary of the items fixed or improved upon in this update.




                      REGIONAL DISABLING OF SLEEVE SYSTEMS
Some sleeve systems, that are not yet available in all geographical regions are now disabled
for users in these regions. This currently affects the following systems in North America:

   •   Anthogyr Pin sleeve 2.0
   •   bredent medical pro guide fixation pin sleeve
   •   Medentika MedentiGuide
   •   Straumann Korea Guided Surgery
   •   YDM Clamp Guided
