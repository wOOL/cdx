Release Notes 9.2.2

Webshop connection

A link to the coDiagnostiX webshop was added to menu and toolbar. It's only visible if the webshop is available in your region or country. (#3450)

Virtual Planning Export

Offsets for Straumann Implant Kits automatically suggested if DWOS XOrder is selected for export (#3396)

Print reports

To increase the ease of use, coDiagnostiX stores the selected printer, paper type, and orientation after printing a report. (#3417)

Viewer Mode if license expired

coDiagnostiX datasets can be viewed in read-only mode if the licence is expired. (#3383)

Other

In addition, this update includes over 35 other improvements and bugfixes.
