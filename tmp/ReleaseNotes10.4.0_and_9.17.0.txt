  Release Notes 10.4 & 9.17
We’re constantly working to improve your experience with coDiagnostiX. Below you will find a
summary of the items fixed or improved upon in this update.


                                      NEW SLEEVE SYSTEMS
New sleeve systems for Anthogyr, Neodent, Bredent and Thommen are available.

                           SURGICAL GUIDE DESIGN IMPROVEMENTS
The following improvements for surgical guide designs sent to Straumann production
centers were made to optimize speed and accuracy of the production process.

Guide type: Users of coDiagnostiX 10.4 are now asked whether their guide is intended for cutting
bone.

Accessory selection: When placing their order, users can now choose between the three options “No
Accessories”, “Sleeves only” or “Sleeves and pins” depending on their current needs.

Customer number: users are now asked to enter their costumer number when placing their order to
ensure the order is correctly assigned to its intended recipient.

                                 CALIBRATION MATRIX PRINTING

We have made enhancements to the process of printing a calibration matrix. Users can now select
which printer they are using, to ensure that the calibration matrix is generated in a way which is
optimized for that printer, for example when using a RapidShape printer, or other DLP printers.
